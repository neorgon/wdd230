Overview

Purpose

  • Create a Website in order to show group of businesses that work together to strengthen the business interests of the group.
  • Create a Website to show services or products in Tiquipaya town.

Scenarios

  • Someone needs to find some information about some company or their services or their products.
  • A market chief from x company needs promote their company on certain category.
  • Z company need a list to all provider from T services in the area.
